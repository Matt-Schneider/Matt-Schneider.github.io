Traditional air cooling is buckling under the intense heat generated by high-density AI hardware, making advanced liquid cooling essential.

(% of Data Centers)

Liquid cooling is gaining significant traction. Without it, AI hardware risks thermal throttling, performance degradation, and premature failure.
