Context & Challenge

The Legacy: Founded around 1860, Guardian Life is a mutual company with deep roots. However, Dean Del Vecchio (CIO) inherited a massive technical debt.

The 1967 Problem

The core policy administration system was implemented in 1967â€”two years before the moon landing.

The Goal: Transform from a legacy insurance carrier into a modern, enterprise digital-facing company in a highly regulated industry.

1. Workplace & Culture Shift

Before technology could change, the environment had to change. The "gray walls and high cubes" were replaced to foster innovation.

Agile SAFe

Adopted the Scaled Agile Framework. 2,500+ employees trained on the new operating model.

Physical Space

Moved to open, airy designs with formal and informal collaboration spaces.

2. The "Production First" Cloud Strategy

Unlike many companies that start with dev/test, Guardian took a "Production First" approach to force enterprise readiness.

Security & Compliance

Partnered deeply with AWS to use Amazon GuardDuty (threat detection) and Amazon Macie (data privacy) to meet regulatory needs.

The "Lights Out" Moment

On November 5, 2018, Dean pressed the button to shut down the last owned data center. No more "racking and stacking."

3. Business Outcomes

  • Launched GuardianDirect.com (DTC platform)
  • M&A Agility: New acquisitions migrate directly to AWS platform
  • 40+ SaaS providers, but strictly AWS for infrastructure
  • Future Focus: AI, AR, VR, and NLP for customer experience
