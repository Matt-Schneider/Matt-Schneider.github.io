Your 90-Day Action Plan

Transforming your organization is a journey, not a single event. Here is a practical, phased 90-day plan to help you start moving from vision to reality, building momentum with concrete actions and early wins.

First 30 Days: Diagnose & Assemble

  • Establish a cross-functional Transformation Team.
  • Conduct an I&O maturity assessment and gap analysis.
  • Begin quantifying the organization's technical debt.

Next 30 Days: Align & Plan

  • Host alignment workshops with business leaders.
  • Identify a high-impact pilot project for a platform approach.
  • Build the business case for key enabling technology.

Final 30 Days: Execute & Communicate

  • Launch the pilot project with clear success metrics.
  • Define and socialize the new I&O scorecard.
  • Launch an internal campaign to share the vision and early wins.
